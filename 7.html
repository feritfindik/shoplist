Pulse Pro is smaller, often quieter in stage 1, with comparable or faster inflation for mid‑size SUPs.

Similar footprint category, Pulse Pro matches/exceeds speed; like others, limited for sustained 20 PSI use.

12V plug‑in pumps

Corded options are better for heavy, repeated jobs; Pulse Pro wins on cordless convenience.

Tips

  • Bulk fill on low stage, then high‑pressure to target PSI—let auto‑off finish.
  • Use a 30W+ USB‑C PD charger; low‑power phone bricks are slow.
  • Allow cooldown between repeated 20 PSI runs to reduce heat stress.
  • If seal is tricky, swap gaskets or use your hand‑pump gasket.
  • Run it from the included dry bag to keep sand out and reduce perceived noise.
  • Add a simple inlet filter on sandy beaches to avoid debris intake.
  • Tighten adapters fully to avoid leaks before inflating.
